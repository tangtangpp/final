A Warm Welcome

Welcome to Shrewsbury International School Bangkok Riverside; an international day school for boys and girls aged 3-18 years, located in an inspirational riverside setting in the heart of Bangkok.

Since 2003, our students' achievements in the examination hall, classroom, sports field, studios and stage, have established a reputation as Thailand's leading international school.
